My Hometown, Jackson

As of 2023, Jackson has a population of roughly 31,000 citizens ("Jackson, MI | Data USA", n.d.). In 1829, Jackson was incorporated into Michigan ("Jackson | Michigan, Map, History, & Facts | Britannica.", n.d.). Jackson is located in south-central Michigan, only roughly an hour drive away from the Michigan southern border. The city of Jackson is an urbanized area. The median income per household is roughly $42,000, which is significantly lower than the median statewide household income of about $42,000 ("Jackson MI | Data USA", n.d.).

Jackson Landmarks

The Cascades

A waterfall attraction that is illuminated and choreographed to entertain the masses. The Cascades are surrounded by a beautiful park with a golf course, playground, splash pad, and ice cream shop ("Cascade Falls (Jackson, Michigan).", 2024).

Ella Sharp Museum

A museum that showcases the history of Jackson with the help of a planetarium, a schoolhouse, and a farmhouse. Offering classes, art, and concerts, Ella Sharp Museum is a key part in experiencing everything Jackson has ("Ella Sharp Museum.", 2025)

The First Michigan State Prison

The first state prison of Michigan resides in Jackson. It was the largest walled prison in the world for a time. The prison housed over 2,000 prisoners and had to close due to overcrowding (“Prison Tour - Jackson, Michigan.”, 2024).
